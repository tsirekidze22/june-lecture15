Why scroll animations matter

Animations triggered on scroll guide the reader's attention, giving each piece of content its moment rather than overwhelming the page at once.

AOS (Animate On Scroll) makes this effortless - drop a single data attribute onto any element and it reveals itself as the viewport reaches it.

Below you'll see four cards. Odd-numbered cards lead with the image on the left; even-numbered cards flip the image to the right. Each card uses a different AOS animation so you can compare the effects.
